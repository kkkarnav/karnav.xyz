Hi! I'm Karnav, a data scientist and software engineer from Calcutta, India.

I'm currently a fourth-year undergraduate at Ashoka University. I studied Computer Science and Economics, and am writing my thesis on climate modelling and land valuation under Prof. Sandeep Juneja. I'm currently working on projects at the Digitalisation & AI Centre and contributing to PuzzleMe.

My research interests are in climate modelling, satellite imaging, digitalisation & privacy, and state capacity.
I've previously worked with Samagra, CEDA, and TCPD.
